ABOUT PATHWAYHUB

Supporting BCIT New Media students through peer-created resources, honest insights, and community connection.

OUR MISSION

PathwayHub was born from a simple realization: navigating the New Media and Web Development program at BCIT can be overwhelming without the right guidance and support.

We're a student-led initiative focused on creating resources by students, for students. Our goal is to bridge the gap between program expectations and reality, offering honest insights and practical advice that you won't find in official course descriptions.

Through peer support, resource sharing, and community building, we're making the BCIT New Media journey more accessible and less intimidating for everyone.
