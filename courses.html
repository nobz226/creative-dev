Course Breakdown

Honest insights into each course from students who've been there, including tips for success and what you'll actually learn.

Year 1 • Term 1

Web Development 1

MDIA 1620

Your introduction to HTML, CSS, and the fundamentals of web development. Expect to build several static websites.

Difficulty:
Beginner

Student Tips:

  • • Practice coding every day, even if just 30 minutes
  • • Don't skip the reading assignments
  • • Ask questions early and often
Year 1 • Term 1

Digital Design

MDIA 1040

Design fundamentals including color theory, typography, and composition using Adobe Creative Suite.

Difficulty:
Moderate

Student Tips:

  • • Study design everywhere - apps, websites, posters
  • • Master Photoshop shortcuts early
  • • Keep a design inspiration collection
Year 1 • Term 2

Web Development 2

MDIA 2109

JavaScript fundamentals, DOM manipulation, and your first interactive web applications.

Difficulty:
Challenging

Student Tips:

  • • JavaScript concepts take time - be patient
  • • Use console.log() liberally for debugging
  • • Work through problems step by step
Year 1 • Term 2

Project Management

MDIA 1128

Learn project planning, client communication, and workflow management essential for creative projects.

Difficulty:
Easy

Student Tips:

  • • Apply concepts to your other course projects
  • • Actually use the tools taught (Asana, Slack, etc.)
  • • Take client communication seriously
Year 2 • Term 1

Advanced JavaScript

MDIA 3126

ES6+, APIs, frameworks like React or Vue, and building complex interactive applications.

Difficulty:
Very Hard

Student Tips:

  • • Review JS fundamentals before term starts
  • • Build personal projects to practice concepts
  • • Form study groups - you'll need them
Year 2 • Term 2

Backend Development

MDIA 3280

Node.js, databases, APIs, and server-side development. Your introduction to full-stack thinking.

Difficulty:
Hard

Student Tips:

  • • Understand the request-response cycle first
  • • Practice SQL queries outside of class
  • • Use Postman to test your APIs
