Program Overview

The New Media and Web Development program at BCIT is an intensive, hands-on journey that prepares you for careers in digital media, web development, and creative technology.

Over two years, you'll master everything from design principles and user experience to front-end and back-end development, working on real projects that build your professional portfolio.

What to Expect

Year One: Foundation Building

Focus on core skills and fundamental concepts. Expect to be challenged but supported as you build your technical foundation.

  • • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals
  • • Design principles and Adobe Creative Suite
  • • Version control with Git
  • • First client project experience
  • • Portfolio development begins

Year Two: Specialization & Polish

Deep dive into your chosen specialization while completing major projects and preparing for industry.

  • • Advanced frameworks and technologies
  • • Capstone project with real client
  • • Industry networking and job preparation
  • • Professional portfolio completion
  • • Internship opportunities
